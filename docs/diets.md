# Diet Guides

## Omnivore
- Staples: chicken breast, eggs, Greek yogurt, beans, rice, oats, potatoes, leafy greens, olive oil.
- Avoid: excess added sugar, ultra-processed snacks, trans fats.
- Notes: balance plate with protein + veg + carbs + healthy fat.

## Vegetarian
- Staples: tofu/tempeh, paneer, beans, lentils, chickpeas, eggs (ovo), dairy (lacto), nuts, seeds, whole grains.
- Avoid: meat, poultry, fish. Check rennet/gelatin for hidden animal products.
- Notes: combine legumes + grains for complete protein; watch B12, iron.

## Vegan
- Staples: tofu/tempeh, seitan, legumes, lentils, chickpeas, soy milk, oat milk, nuts, seeds, whole grains, veggies.
- Avoid: all animal products (meat, fish, eggs, dairy, honey, gelatin).
- Notes: prioritize iron + B12-fortified foods; include omega-3 sources (chia, flax).

## Pescatarian
- Staples: fish (salmon, tuna, cod), seafood, eggs, dairy, beans, lentils, whole grains, veggies, olive oil.
- Avoid: meat and poultry.
- Notes: balance oily and lean fish; watch mercury for tuna (1–2x/week).

## Keto
- Staples: eggs, fatty fish, meat, cheese, Greek yogurt (full fat), avocado, nuts, seeds, leafy greens, olive/coconut oil.
- Avoid: sugar, bread, rice, pasta, most fruit (small berries ok), starchy veg (potato, corn).
- Notes: aim <30–50g net carbs/day; keep electrolytes (sodium/potassium/magnesium) up.

## Paleo
- Staples: meat, fish, eggs, veggies, fruit, nuts, seeds, olive/coconut oil, sweet potato.
- Avoid: grains, legumes, dairy, refined sugar, seed oils.
- Notes: focus on minimally processed foods; tubers are generally ok.
