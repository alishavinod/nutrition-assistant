# Nutrition FAQ (for RAG)

- How do I increase protein on a budget? Use eggs, canned tuna, chicken thighs, Greek yogurt, lentils, tofu; batch-cook and freeze portions; buy in bulk when on sale.
- How many meals per day? 3–4 works for most; spread protein evenly (20–40g per meal).
- Fast breakfast ideas? Greek yogurt with berries, overnight oats with protein powder, eggs + spinach, cottage cheese + fruit.
- What should I swap for dairy-free? Use soy/pea milk, coconut yogurt, olive oil instead of butter, nutritional yeast for cheesy flavor.
- Keto snack ideas? Boiled eggs, cheese sticks, nuts/seeds, deli turkey, olives, cucumber with cream cheese.
- Vegan protein sources? Tofu, tempeh, seitan, lentils, chickpeas, edamame, pea/soy protein powder.
- How to handle allergies? Always exclude allergens; watch cross-contamination; check labels for hidden ingredients (e.g., soy in sauces, nuts in pesto).
- Meal-prep rules? Cook protein + carb + veg separately, portion into containers, chill fast, reheat once; label date; keep 3–4 days refrigerated.
- Hitting fiber? Add beans, lentils, chia/flax, berries, oats, veggies; increase water when adding fiber.
- Quick cooking methods? Sheet-pan (20–30 min), stir-fry (10–15 min), pressure cooker for beans/grains, microwave steam veggies (3–5 min).
